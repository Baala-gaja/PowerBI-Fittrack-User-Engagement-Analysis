# FitTrack User Engagement Analytics Dashboard

This project was developed during my Business Analyst Internship (Feb 2026).

The dashboard analyzes user engagement, retention behavior, AI adoption, and churn risk for a fitness application using Power BI.

## Key Dashboards

1. Executive Engagement Dashboard
2. Retention & Stickiness Analysis
3. AI Impact & Upgrade Readiness
4. Habit Formation & User Segmentation
5. Churn Risk & Growth Strategy
6. Growth & Unit Economics

## Key Insights

• Strong early activation but retention drops after Day 4  
• 60% of users fall into high churn risk category  
• AI users show higher workout engagement  
• Majority users remain casual users without habit formation  

## Tools Used

Power BI  
Excel  
Data Modelling  
Business Analytics  

## Business Value

The dashboard helps product teams understand:

• user engagement trends  
• churn risk segments  
• AI feature adoption  
• monetization readiness

This enables data-driven product strategy and growth decisions.
